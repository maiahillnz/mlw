Maddie's Last Walk

Chapter One

Hi! You must be new here! I'm Clover! What's your name? Where are you from? Has anyone shown you around yet? You have nice eyes! Wanna play?"

Maddie wagged her tail a little, unsure of what to think of the fluffy golden ball of energy bouncing around her, asking a million different questions without a breath of space for answers.

"Umm" she ventured, "I'm Maddie and while it's very nice to meet you, I think I must be having a dream so I'll be going home soon." She paused, amusedly whatching Clover frollicking around. "Well, I guess I can play for a while..."

With that, Clover yelped with joy and bounded off through the field they were playing on to go splashing into a cool, slow running stream

Maddie took off after her, amazed at how she could run in her dreams, just like the puppy she used be, tearing around the park chasing birds and anything else that caught her eye.

"Well, I'll make the most of it while I can!" she thought as she closed the gap between her and Clover.
